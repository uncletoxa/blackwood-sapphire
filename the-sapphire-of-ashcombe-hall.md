# The Sapphire at Ashcombe Hall

Six masked guests arrived at Ashcombe Hall. Each occupied a different room, carried a different object, ordered a different drink, arrived at a different time, and was associated with
different evidence.

The guest associated with the Blackwood Sapphire was the thief.

Guests: Ada, Basil, Celeste, Dorian, Evelyn, Felix
Rooms: Ballroom, Conservatory, Gallery, Kitchen, Library, Study
Objects: brass key, glass cutter, lockpick, screwdriver, silk cord, wax seal
Drinks: champagne, coffee, lemonade, mineral water, sherry, tea
Evidence: Blackwood Sapphire, blue thread, candle wax, muddy footprint, silver button, torn note
Times: 8:00, 8:20, 8:40, 9:00, 9:20, 9:40
